What We Do

The PTSA supports the school through communications (email listserve), programming (Peaceable Schools, enrichment programs), and fundraising.
New initiatives include a monthly Parents' Club adoration hour and service project to promote family volunteerism.
Did you know that in just the past few years, the PTSA has funded over $125,000 of items for the school, faculty, and new facilities?
St. Matthew families can help by purchasing gift certificates through Scrip, signing up for store rebate programs, and clipping Box Tops. 10 easy ways you can raise money for your school

Meetings

PTSA meets during the school year, once a month, on Wednesday nights at 6:30pm in the St Mark room in the Parish Center.
Join us and find out how you can get involved in our mission.
Childcare will be offered in the church nursery.
To add an item to our meeting agenda please contact the president in advance of the meeting or attend the meeting and present your agenda item.
The PTSA invites you to attend Mass with other St Matthew School families.
Mass will be held in Feeney Hall one Thursday each month at 7am.
