Easy ways to earn money for your school

Box Tops for Education

Clip tops and earn cash for St. Matthew School! Find Box Tops coupons on hundreds of your favorite products. Each Box Top coupon is worth 10¢ for our school. Clip and send the Box Tops to school. Our school will collect the Box Tops and send them to Box Tops for Education.
Twice each year, Box Tops will send a check to our school for each Box Tops coupon redeemed, up to $20,000 each year. We will have a contest each semester to see which class can collect the most Box Tops, and the winning class will get a pizza party!

Max Dollars For Education

You can help our school earn Max Dollars by using your Max Card each time you shop at County Market or Cub Foods from August 24 — mid-October. This program does not cost anything, and will not take away from your personal Max Rewards you are already earning. Registration forms are available at your local County Market and only take a minute to complete (Note: You must re-register your Max Card each year). Then use your Max Card every time you shop. It's that easy. We will be registering cards at Fee Day!

Scrip

When you purchase scrip, you are purchasing negotiable gift certificates that are used just like cash. The national and local retailers issue the scrip where many of you already shop. You can use scrip to purchase everyday expenses like clothing, gas, and other essentials. They also make great Christmas and birthday gifts. Orders will be made once a month (check the school calendar for dates). With every purchase you make, we earn revenue for our school.

Schnucks eScrip

Customers in Savoy, Champaign and Urbana who are presently using a Schnucks Customer Card for Fuel Rewards may register that card to earn eScrip contributions by calling (800) 931-6258 or registering online. The Schnucks eScrip Customer Card may be used to generate both eScrip contributions and Fuel Rewards, where available. The more you shop the more we can earn! Online enrollment and more information

Tell your friends and family about the programs above and help us earn even more money for St. Matthew Catholic School!
